Who am I?

A Difficult Question.

If you ask my friends, then they're likely going to tell you Heng-Wei, but these seven letters only have 7 bytes of information about me, which certainly cannot make you acquainted with me, can it? How about telling my hobits and professional interests, which you should be able in the main page, the Research, and the Notes page? Maybe, not really?

Would my actions, with their cost and prizes, tell you who I could be, which are partly summarized in my CV? Would my wills, which possibly could only be observed via myself and might be revealed in Articles, be helpful in this problem? Would the combination of all of these do?

At this point, you could have seize some portion of me with the above texts, but not by the information itself provided by these paragraphs. And some enjoy with these, while some get really annoyed by them. But, at the end of the day, we have to choose the companion, with whom we can go on, in order to resolve the biological, or chemical, sense of solitude holding communities in the social world tight that (temporarily?) wins Homo sapiens the game of evolution.

Please feel free to email me if you have similar thoughts.
