My Research Interests

In general, I am interested in the triple point and interfaces between high energy physics, cosmology, and mathematics.

MORE

Theoretical Particle Physics

The study of theoretical particle physics concerns modelling fundamental particles and their coupling with each other. Some care more about modelling, while some care more about fundamental. I'd lay in the intersection.

MORE

Cosmology

Cosmology might be the study of where the world came, where world are now, and where the world are bound to. By the world, I mean a Cauchy surface an event lies on. Dark energy and fine-tunings are the problems.

MORE

Classical and Quantum Gravity

The classical one is just geometric analysis in disguise, but the quantum one might be the last puzzle of physics. Some say the difficulty is the non-renormalizability of the theory, but there is much more than that.

MORE

Mathematical Physics

Some say it's the mathematical study on physical models, but some say it's physical study with mathematical consideration. But it's the intersection of physics and mathematics that interests me.
